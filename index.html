Ramin Ghorbani

PhD candidate @Pattern Recognition Lab, TU Delft

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Pattern Recognition group at the Delft University of Technology, working with dr. David Tax and Prof.dr.ir Marcel Reinders. My research is about Representation Learning, mainly focusing on learning from the limited labeled data. I'm interested in uncovering the structure of limited labeled data using Self-Supervised Representation Learning to improve the performance of predictive models for different downstream tasks.
